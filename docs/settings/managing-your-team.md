Rayt supports unlimited team seats on every plan — we don't believe charging per seat is a great way to encourage collaboration. Here's how to get your team onboarded.

## Inviting teammates

From **Settings → Team**, click "Invite" and enter the new teammate's email. You'll pick a role (Admin, Editor, or Viewer) and optionally restrict their access to specific stores if you run multiple.

They'll receive an invite email; once they accept, they show up in your team list immediately.

## Understanding roles

**Admin** can do everything — billing, integrations, team management, and review moderation. Reserve this for people you fully trust.

**Editor** can moderate reviews, reply publicly, edit campaigns, and update widget settings. They can't touch billing or remove teammates.

**Viewer** is read-only. Perfect for an exec or stakeholder who wants to see analytics without being able to change anything.

## Multi-store access

If you manage reviews for multiple stores under a single Rayt account, you can scope each teammate to specific stores. A freelance moderator might only see Store A, while your CEO sees everything.

Scopes are set per teammate in the same Team settings panel. You can adjust them at any time and changes take effect immediately — no sign-out required.
