Billing lives at **Settings → Billing**. You don't need to be an Admin to view invoices, but you do need Admin access to change payment details.

## Changing your payment method

Click "Update card" in the billing panel. We use Stripe for payments, so you'll see a secure Stripe-hosted card form — Rayt never touches your card details directly. The new card is charged on your next renewal; we don't re-bill your current invoice.

If you're paying via invoice (annual plans), contact support to update bank details — this can't be done self-serve.

## Updating invoice metadata

If your accounting team needs a specific company name, tax ID, or billing address on invoices, you can set these in **Settings → Billing → Invoice details**. Changes take effect on the next invoice issued; we don't retroactively update past invoices.

For VAT reverse-charge (EU customers), add your VAT number here and we'll apply the reverse-charge rules automatically.

## Downloading past invoices

Every invoice Rayt has ever issued to your account is available in the Billing panel. Click the download icon next to any invoice to get a PDF — these are final PDFs suitable for your records or expense software.

If you need invoices older than 2 years or a specific format (e.g., a CSV export of all invoices for audit), email support and we'll package them up for you.
