Rayt's pricing scales with the number of orders you process each month. This guide explains how the tiers differ and how to choose one without overpaying.

## Understanding the tiers

Our Starter plan is designed for stores with fewer than 200 monthly orders — it includes core review requests, a product review widget, and basic email branding. Growth adds photo and video reviews, incentives, and priority moderation tools. Pro unlocks advanced syndication, site-wide displays, and our review-import tool for migrations.

Every plan includes unlimited team seats and unlimited reviews imported from other platforms, so you won't pay more just for collaborators.

## When to upgrade

You should consider moving up a tier when any of these become true: you're consistently sending more review requests than your current allotment; you want to collect photo/video reviews from customers; or you need to push reviews to Google Shopping, Meta, or other syndication targets.

Rayt will prompt you in-app when your usage exceeds the plan limits — no surprises on your invoice.

## Changing plans later

You can move between plans at any time from **Settings → Billing**. Upgrades take effect immediately and we prorate the difference on your next invoice. Downgrades take effect at the end of your current billing period so you don't lose access to features you've already paid for.

If you're still unsure, our team can run you through a quick discovery call — just reach out via the help widget in the bottom right.
