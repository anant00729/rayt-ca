Moving from Yotpo? You won't leave any reviews behind. Rayt's migration tool pulls in every review you've collected — text, photos, dates, and star ratings — and maps them to the right products automatically.

## Exporting from Yotpo

Log in to your Yotpo dashboard, open **Moderation → Export**, and request a full review export. Yotpo emails you a CSV within a few minutes. You want the format that includes product IDs, review text, ratings, author names, and submission timestamps — that's the default export.

Keep the file handy; we'll upload it directly into Rayt.

## Importing into Rayt

Inside Rayt, navigate to **Settings → Import Reviews** and select "From Yotpo." Drop in the CSV you just downloaded. Rayt will show a preview of the first 20 rows so you can confirm the product mapping looks right before committing.

Once you click **Start Import**, the job runs in the background. Depending on volume this takes anywhere from a few seconds to a half hour. We'll email you when it's done.

## What happens after

All imported reviews keep their original submission date, so your product pages continue to show "reviewed 2 years ago" correctly. Photos and videos are rehosted on Rayt's CDN so your Yotpo account can be safely closed afterwards.

If anything looks off — duplicate reviews, missing products, mismatched ratings — contact support and we'll re-run the import with corrected mapping.
