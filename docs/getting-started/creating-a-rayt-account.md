Welcome to Rayt! Before you can start collecting reviews, you'll need a Rayt account. This short guide walks you through the sign-up flow and what to expect after your first login.

## Signing up

Head over to the Rayt sign-up page and enter your business email, a secure password, and your store URL. If you already have Shopify, BigCommerce, or WooCommerce, Rayt will detect your platform automatically so you can skip most of the manual setup later.

We'll send a verification email within a minute or two. Click the link inside to confirm your address — this protects your account and unlocks the rest of the onboarding flow.

## Connecting your store

Once you're signed in, the first thing Rayt will ask for is a store connection. For Shopify merchants this takes a single click via our official app. For other platforms you'll be guided through adding an API key and confirming the domain you want reviews pulled from.

If anything looks off during the connection, don't worry — you can disconnect and reconnect at any time from **Settings → Stores**.

## Inviting your team

Reviews are rarely managed by one person. From your dashboard, open **Team** and invite collaborators by email. You can set each teammate to Admin, Editor, or Viewer depending on how much access they need. Editors can reply to reviews and tweak campaigns; Viewers get a read-only window into performance.

That's it — your account is ready. Next, take a look at [Selecting a plan](/docs/getting-started/selecting-a-plan) to pick the tier that matches your review volume.
