Your customers are already writing your best ad copy — they just don't know it. AI Convert takes your highest-rated reviews and turns them into ready-to-post social content in seconds.

## What is AI Convert?

AI Convert scans your incoming reviews, identifies the ones with strong sentiment and specific product praise, then generates branded ad creatives you can post directly to Instagram, TikTok, or Facebook.

![AI Convert dashboard overview](/blog/img_1.png)

No design skills required. No copywriter needed. Just reviews → ads.

## Step 1: Enable AI Convert

Go to **AI Convert** in your RayT sidebar. On first launch you'll see the setup wizard:

1. Connect your brand kit (logo, colors, fonts)
2. Choose which review sources to pull from
3. Set a minimum star rating threshold (we recommend ≥ 4 stars)

Click **Activate** when done.

## Step 2: Review the Generated Creatives

RayT generates a new batch of ad creatives every 24 hours. Each card shows:

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| Review excerpt | The snippet used as ad copy |
| Product | Which product the review is for |
| Format | Square (1:1), Story (9:16), or Banner (16:9) |
| Score | AI confidence score for ad effectiveness |

You can approve, edit, or discard each creative before it goes live.

## Step 3: Publish or Schedule

Hit **Publish** to post immediately, or pick a date and time to schedule. RayT connects directly to:

- Instagram Business
- Facebook Pages
- TikTok for Business

> **Tip:** Creatives with a specific product name and a measurable outcome ("reduced my acne in 2 weeks") consistently outperform generic praise. Use the **Score** column to prioritize these.

## Performance Tracking

Once your ads are live, head to **Analytics → Ad Performance** to see:

- Impressions and reach per creative
- Click-through rate vs. your store average
- Revenue attributed to review-based ads

Ads generated from reviews with 5 stars and 50+ words tend to see **2.4× higher CTR** than standard product ads.
