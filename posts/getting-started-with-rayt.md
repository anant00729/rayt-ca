RayT makes it easy to collect, manage, and display product reviews on your Shopify store. This guide walks you through installation and your first review campaign.

## Prerequisites

- A Shopify store (any plan)
- Admin access to your store

## Step 1: Install RayT

1. Visit the [Shopify App Store](https://apps.shopify.com) and search for **RayT**.
2. Click **Add app** and follow the OAuth prompts.
3. Once installed, you'll be redirected to the RayT dashboard.

![RayT App Store listing](/blog/img_1.png)

## Step 2: Enable Review Widgets

Head to **Widgets** in the sidebar and choose which review display you want:

- **Star Rating** — shows aggregate stars below your product title
- **Reviews List** — a full scrollable list of customer reviews
- **Review Summary** — an AI-generated summary of recent feedback

Toggle the widget on and click **Save**.

![Widget configuration panel](https://placehold.co/740x400?text=Widget+Config)

## Step 3: Send Your First Review Request

Navigate to **Customers → Review Requests** and click **New Campaign**. Choose:

- Which products to request reviews for
- The email template (or use the default)
- The send delay after purchase (7 days is recommended)

Click **Launch** and RayT handles the rest.

## Importing Existing Reviews

Already have reviews on another platform? RayT can import from Judge.me, Stamped, or Yotpo in one click.

Go to **Settings → Import Reviews**, select your source, and authorize the connection. Imported reviews keep their original dates and star ratings.

## What to Expect

After your first campaign launches:

| Timeframe | What happens |
|-----------|-------------|
| Day 0 | Campaign created, emails queued |
| Day 7 | Review request emails sent |
| Day 8–14 | First reviews start arriving |
| Day 30 | Enough data for AI summaries |

> **Tip:** Stores that send review requests within 10 days of delivery see a 3× higher response rate than those that wait longer.
